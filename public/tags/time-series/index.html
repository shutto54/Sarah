Time Series

Posted on 0001.01.01 · Tagged in r, time series

The x axis represents time, while the y is variable of your choice. First we load the libraries: library(Lahman) library(sqldf) library(devtools) library(ggplot2) #extracting the data query<-"SELECT yearID,HR FROM Batting WHERE playerID='ruthba01'" result<-sqldf(query) #visualizing the data ggplot()+ geom_point(data=result,aes(x=yearID,y=HR))+ geom_line(data=result,aes(x=yearID,y=HR))+ ggtitle("Ruth's Homerun Totals Through the Years")+ xlab("Year")+ ylab("Homeruns") #if you do not want to see the points, comment out the geom_point line #then leave geom_line and then you have yourself a line graph…
